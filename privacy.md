MoshMux Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

MoshMux (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

MoshMux is designed to be a lightweight, privacy-focused terminal application.

We do not collect or store personal data on our servers.

However, the app may process the following information locally on your device:

Connection details (e.g., server address, session state)
Terminal session data (for maintaining persistent sessions)

This information is stored locally and is not transmitted to us.

2. Remote Connections

MoshMux connects to remote servers using technologies such as mosh and tmux.

All data transmitted between your device and your server is:

Encrypted
Controlled by your own infrastructure

We do not have access to your remote sessions.

3. Analytics

MoshMux does not use third-party analytics and does not track user behavior.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

5. Security

We prioritize security by:

Using secure communication protocols
Avoiding unnecessary data collection
6. Children’s Privacy

MoshMux does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL.
